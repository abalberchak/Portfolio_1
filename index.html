ABOUT

The tale of an Industrial Engineer turned Full-Stack Developer...

Andrew (Andy) grew up in San Diego, CA and studied Industrial Engineering at Cal Poly SLO where processes are dissected, evaluated, and streamlined using lean tools. In other words, the art of 'doing more with less'. While working as a Solutions Engineer at Oracle and later an Operations Analyst at Zenefits, he became exposed to the rapidly growing technologies of Silicon Valley. To this day, he has put in 1500+ hours mastering the art of designing beautiful and fluid webpages to bring online communities together.

Today, Andy is a passionate Full-stack Developer who uses his expertise in lean practices and software development to provide consultative advice and solutions to a variety of audiences. When not working, Andy enjoys spending his time outdoors at any of California's beautiful beaches or hiking in one of the lush trails that surrounds the area. On quieter days, he enjoys producing and mixing electronic music on FL Studio 12 while hanging out with his small parrot, Chester.

TIMELINE

CAL POLY SLO

2008 - 2013

Cal Poly San Luis Obispo is well-known for its excellent engineering program and its vast natural beauties surrounding the area. Andy graduated Cal Poly with a B.S. Industrial Engineering while being involved in the Sales Engineering Club where he picked up fundamentals of business acumen and public speaking.

ORACLE CORPORATION

2013 - 2015

Thanks to his involvement in the Sales Engineering Club, Andy landed his first gig as a Solutions Engineer where he used problem solving skills to uncover potential client bottlenecks, prepare POCs with front-end development, and lead technical product demonstrations. It was here that jumpstarted his journey to the incredible world of coding as he continued building mock websites for each of his customers' demos.

ZENEFITS

2015 - 2016

After working for nearly 2 years as a Solutions Engineer, Andy decided to give a shot at Operations and dive into the world of startups. He joined this Silicon Valley Unicorn to use his Industrial Engineering experience to help alleviate internal workflows while supporting a variety of teams to ensure customers had an excellent experience while using Zenefits as their new Insurance broker. He also continued building front-end applications to support the Operations Team in streamlining its internal processes.

CODING DOJO SAN JOSE

2016 - 2017

Zenefits had begun to rebrand itself and was rebuilding itself from the ground up. Andy took this opportunity to dive deeper into the world of software development, a passion that had been growing ever since his first exposure at Oracle. He then decided to embark on a journey where students train 1000+ hours learning 3 full technology stacks including Python, Ruby on Rails, and the MEAN stack. Here, he learned to quickly adapt to a world of rapidly changing technologies.
